Hello! I'm Conner Smith, a passionate technology professional based in Oklahoma City. I recently completed an internship as a Junior Software Engineer and Scrum Master, where I led the development of a Progressive Web App aimed at providing essential EHR and medical caregiving tools for Native American Tribes with limited internet access. With a strong background in cloud computing, web development, and Agile project management, I hold an AWS Solutions Architect - Associate certification and a Hashicorp Certified: Terraform Associate certification. My hands-on experience includes working with AWS services, Docker, MariaDB, and ensuring HIPAA compliance for secure handling of PII. I have a diverse set of interests beyond technology, including music (I play the piano), computer games, cars, Formula 1, board games, Dungeons and Dragons, astronomy, and golf. My passion for cloud technology stems from its essential role in shaping the future of the world and the internet. I believe that many complex problems can be solved using cloud technologies, and I'm eager to contribute to making the world a better place through them. I'm excited about the opportunities ahead and eager to continue my professional growth.
